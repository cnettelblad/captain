# 🌐 Globetrotter 2026

Globetrotter is Wanderlust's yearly travel challenge. Complete challenges, log your submissions, and climb the ranks — earning **Bronze**, **Silver**, and **Gold** medals along the way.

## Usage
```
/globetrotter me
```
Shows your Globetrotter summary:
- Total submissions and points
- Unique non-recurring challenges completed
- Recurring totals (countries, continents, UNESCO sites, Wanderlusters met, Wonders)
- Breakdown by category

## 🏅 Rank Tiers
- 🥉 **Bronze** — 100 + points
- 🥈 **Silver** — 200 + points
- 🥇 **Gold** — 300 + points
